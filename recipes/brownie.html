Overloaded Brownies

Description

These decadent chocolate brownies are rich, fudgy, and absolutely irresistible. Perfect for satisfying your sweet tooth, they are easy to make and sure to become a family favorite. Whether you're serving them at a party, bringing them to a potluck, or just enjoying them as a treat at home, these brownies are bound to impress.

Ingredients

  • 226 grams unsalted butter, melted
  • 200 grams granulated sugar
  • 220 grams packed light brown sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 125 grams all-purpose flour
  • 85 grams unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 175 grams semi-sweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a 9x13-inch baking pan with parchment paper or lightly grease it.
  2. Combine the melted butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar in a large bowl, whisking until smooth.
  3. Add the eggs and vanilla extract to the mixture, beating until fully incorporated.
  4. Sift together the flour, cocoa powder, and salt in a separate bowl. Gradually add this dry mixture to the wet ingredients, stirring until just combined.
  5. Fold in the chocolate chips, ensuring they are evenly distributed throughout the batter.
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan and spread it evenly with a spatula.
  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with only a few moist crumbs attached. Be careful not to overbake, as this will result in drier brownies.
  8. Allow the brownies to cool completely in the pan before cutting them into squares.
  9. Enjoy! Serve with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a glass of cold milk for an extra special treat.
